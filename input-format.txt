Initial Input
The initial input is a plain text file of the following form. Fields in lines are separated by white
space.
Line 1. Number of reviewers R. Utility of success. Utility of failure. P(Success).
Lines 2 through R + 1. One line per reviewer. Cost of reviewer. P(R = T|Success). P(R =
T|Failure).
